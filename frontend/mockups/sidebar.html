Why This Structure

Sidebar is only for real destinations

Dashboard, upload, and jobs are enough for Phase 1. No fake reports or settings needed yet.

Top-right becomes identity, not logout

The profile button leaves room for future account actions while reducing visual clutter in the header.

Logout belongs in the footer rail

That keeps navigation at the top and session/account actions anchored in one predictable place.
